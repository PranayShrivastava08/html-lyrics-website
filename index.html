ROCKSTAR

Tum Ho Song- Rockstar Movie

Rockstar is a 2011 Indian Hindi-language musical romantic drama written and directed by Imtiaz Ali. The film stars Ranbir Kapoor and Nargis Fakhri in lead roles, with Aditi Rao Hydari, Piyush Mishra, Shernaz Patel, Kumud Mishra and Shammi Kapoor in pivotal supporting roles. The soundtrack was composed by A. R. Rahman. The film marks the posthumous film appearance of Shammi Kapoor, following his death on 14 August 2011
